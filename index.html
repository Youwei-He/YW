PhD Candidate in Surrogate based optimization method and its application

I am currently a PhD researcher in Fluid Machinery and Engineering at Xi'an Jiaotong University, and expect to graduate Winter 2020. My research focuses on leveraging surrogate models for data-efficient modeling and optimization. I work on challenges involving combining different kinds of data and specific sequential decision making techniques. I am very interested in understanding how uncertainty can influence decision making in complex engineering design and particularly in the computational fluid dynamic based applications.
